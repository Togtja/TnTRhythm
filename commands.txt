!play <yt_link> (plays a youtube link)
!play or !resume or !continue (continue a pause song/music)
!pause (pause a song/music)
!repeat or !loop (repeats/loop a song/music until repeat get toggled of or skip is called)
!skip (skip a song/music to the next on in the playlist)
!queue or !playlist (displays the queue/playlist)
!clear (clears the queue/playlist)
!quit (bot leaves the voice chat and clear its playlist)
!log <DEBUG/INFO/WARNING/ERROR/CRITICAL/REMOVE> (log messages about the state of the bot commands in discord, the lower lever includes the logging of higher level (aka INFO logs warning, error and critical but not debug), and REMOVE removes the logger from the channel that called it)
